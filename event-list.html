Music Activities

With an array of styles, our music classes help children explore the power of rhythm, melody, tonality and beat while nurturing key physical, social and intellectual skills. We aim to inspire a profound love and enthusiasm for music by immersing young children in a rich musical environment. In our Music classes, your child will develop and build basic musical, social, emotional, cognitive, and psychomotoric skills through singing, playing, listening and moving to music. Benefits of Music classes involves the following,

  1. it improves academic skills
  2. it refines decipline and patience
  3. it cultivates social skills
  4. it boosts self-esteem

Social Activities

Social activities are a part of the process through which children and young people develop the skills, attitudes, and values necessary to acquire social and emotional competence. We enhance their problem solving attitude. Preschool social skills depend on four abilities:

  1. Self Control
  2. Verbal Communication
  3. Empathy
  4. Concentration

Learning Activities

Your child's first years of school are filled with many wondrous moments. It's a time of tremendous social, emotional, physical, and intellectual development, and it can come and go before you know it. The skills learned at this stage -- knowing what sounds the letter A makes or adding 2 + 2 -- may seem simple but they will set your child up for a lifetime of learning. We teach them skills like,

  1. Letter and sounds
  2. Colors, Shapes and Objects
  3. Cutting and Drawing
  4. Socializing and Sharping
